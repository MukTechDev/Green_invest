What are the benefits of ATMOS?

ATMOS helps create a healthier home environment by combining advanced light therapy and air purification technologies. Its benefits include enhanced sleep quality, stress reduction, healthier skin, an uplifted mood, improved mental well-being, relief from allergy symptoms, cleaner lungs, sharper cognition, and increased productivity, among others. Discover more about the health advantages of our light therapy and air purification features.

How does ATMOS work?

ATMOS combines multiple health-enhancing technologies into an elegant, all-in-one design. Equipped with an efficient motor and multi-layer HEPA filters, it captures and eliminates 99.95% of airborne pollutants, ensuring clean air throughout your home. Its advanced light therapy uses specialized LEDs that emit precise wavelengths, proven to support your health and well-being.
